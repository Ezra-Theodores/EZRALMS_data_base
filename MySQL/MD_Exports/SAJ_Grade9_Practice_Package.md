**Source:** SAJ 01

**Indonesian:** Dalam deret aritmatika, suku pertama adalah 4, suku tengah adalah 22, dan beda adalah 3. Tentukan jumlah deret ini.

**English:** In an arithmetic series, the first term is 4, the middle term is 22, and the common difference is 3. Find the sum of this series.

**Explanation (EN):** $U_t = 22$, $a=4$. $U_t = (a+U_n)/2 \rightarrow 44 = 4+U_n \rightarrow U_n=40$. $U_n = a+(n-1)d \rightarrow 40=4+(n-1)3 \rightarrow n=13$. $S_n = 13 \times 22 = 286$.

## 📖 Persamaan Garis Lurus

### Question 10: Kedudukan Dua Garis

**Source:** SAJ 01

**Indonesian:** Diketahui beberapa persamaan garis berikut:
(i) $2x - 3y = 6$
(ii) $3x - 2y = 8$
(iii) $6x + 4y = 12$
(iv) $4x - 6y = 10$
Pasangan garis yang saling tegak lurus adalah . . . .

**English:** Given the following line equations:
(i) $2x - 3y = 6$
(ii) $3x - 2y = 8$
(iii) $6x + 4y = 12$
(iv) $4x - 6y = 10$
The pair of lines that are perpendicular to each other is . . . .

**Options:**
- A. (i) dan (ii)
- B. (i) dan (iii)
- C. (ii) dan (iii)
- D. (iii) dan (iv)

**Answer:** B

**Explanation (ID):** Dua garis tegak lurus jika $m_1 \times m_2 = -1$.
Gradien garis $Ax + By = C$ adalah $m = -A/B$.
(i) $2x - 3y = 6 \Rightarrow m_1 = -2/(-3) = 2/3$
(iii) $6x + 4y = 12 \Rightarrow m_3 = -6/4 = -3/2$
Perkalian gradien: $2/3 \times (-3/2) = -1$. Jadi, garis (i) tegak lurus dengan (iii).

**Explanation (EN):** Two lines are perpendicular if $m_1 \times m_2 = -1$.
The gradient of $Ax + By = C$ is $m = -A/B$.
(i) $2x - 3y = 6 \Rightarrow m_1 = -2/(-3) = 2/3$
(iii) $6x + 4y = 12 \Rightarrow m_3 = -6/4 = -3/2$
Product of gradients: $2/3 \times (-3/2) = -1$. Thus, line (i) is perpendicular to (iii).
