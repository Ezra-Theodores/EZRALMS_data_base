Rules

📈Positive numbers: above zero (gain, profit, above sea level)
📉Negative numbers: below zero (loss, debt, below sea level)
0️⃣Zero: neither positive nor negative
Adding Integers

When adding integers, we consider the signs!

Same Signs → Add & Keep

(+3) + (+5) = ?
+8
(−3) + (−5) = ?
−8

Different Signs → Subtract & Keep the Sign of the Larger

(+7) + (−3) = ?
+4
(−7) + (+3) = ?
−4

🛒 Shopping Example

Sarah has Rp 50,000. She buys a book for Rp 75,000. Her account balance is: 50,000 + (−75,000) = −25,000. She is Rp 25,000 in debt!

Subtracting Integers

Subtracting an integer is the same as adding its opposite!

a − b = a + (−b)

Examples

(+5) − (+3) = ?
+2
(−5) − (−3) = ?
−2
(+5) − (−3) = ?
+8
(−5) − (+3) = ?
−8

🌡️ Temperature Example

The temperature at 6 AM is +5°C. By noon, it drops 8 degrees. The temperature is: 5 − 8 = −3°C. Brrr, it's below freezing!

💰 Money Example

Andi has Rp 20,000. He owes Budi Rp 30,000. His net worth is: 20,000 + (−30,000) = −10,000. He has a debt of Rp 10,000!

Real Life Applications

🏔️ Altitude

Mount Everest is +8,849m above sea level. The Dead Sea is −430m below sea level. The difference is 8,849 − (−430) = 9,279 meters!

📅 Seasons

In winter, Jakarta might be 32°C, while snowy mountains are −5°C. The difference is 32 − (−5) = 37°C!

📊 Business

A store earned Rp 1,000,000 last month but lost Rp 300,000 this month. Profit: 1,000,000 + (−300,000) = +700,000

🎮 Game Scores

In a game, you gain +50 points for collecting coins but lose −30 points for falling. Net gain: 50 + (−30) = +20 points!

📏 Depth

A submarine is 200m below sea level (−200m). It rises 80m. Now it's at: −200 + 80 = −120m below sea level.
