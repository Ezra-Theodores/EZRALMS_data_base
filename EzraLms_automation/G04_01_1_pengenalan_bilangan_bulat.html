What are Integers?

Integers are whole numbers that include positive numbers, negative numbers, and zero. Unlike fractions or decimals, integers have no parts — they are complete numbers!

Real Life Examples

🌡️ Temperature

In winter, Jakarta might be +32°C (hot). In the mountains, it can be −5°C (cold, below freezing). Temperature uses integers to show how hot or cold it is!

🏔️ Altitude

Mount Everest is +8,849 meters above sea level. The Dead Sea is −430 meters below sea level. Altitude uses integers to measure height and depth!

💰 Money

If you have Rp 50,000 in your account, your balance is +50,000. If you owe someone Rp 30,000, we can write that as −30,000. Integers help us track money!

📊 Business

A store that earns money has +Rp 1,000,000 (profit). A store that loses money has −Rp 300,000 (loss). Integers help businesses track their success!

🎮 Game Scores

In some games, you can have a score of +500 points (ahead) or −200 points (behind). Integers make it easy to see who's winning!

📏 Floor Numbers

In some countries, ground floor is 0, floors above are +1, +2, +3... Floors below ground (parking) are −1, −2, −3... Buildings use integers too!

Practice Quiz

Q1. Which is a positive integer?

Q4. The temperature is −3°C and rises to +5°C. What happened?
