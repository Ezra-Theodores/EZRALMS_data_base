Comparison Symbols

We use special symbols to compare numbers. These symbols help us show which number is bigger, smaller, or equal!

>
Greater Than
<
Less Than
=
Equal To

Memory Tips

🍎 The big open part faces the GREATER number. 5 > 3 means 5 is greater than 3!
🐊 Think of it like a hungry crocodile. The crocodile's mouth opens toward the bigger number!
Comparison Rules

Comparing Positive Numbers

📈On the number line, the number on the RIGHT is always GREATER. So 5 > 3 because 5 is to the right of 3.

Comparing Negative Numbers

❄️For negative numbers, the number closer to zero is GREATER. So -3 > -7 because -3 is closer to zero than -7.

Comparing Positive and Negative

☀️Every POSITIVE number is greater than ZERO. Every NEGATIVE number is less than ZERO.
🌡️So +5 > 0 > -5. Positive numbers are greater than negative numbers!
Examples

Positive vs Positive

5 > 3 5 is greater than 3
2 < 8 2 is less than 8

Negative vs Negative

-3 > -7 -3 is greater than -7
-5 < -1 -5 is less than -1

Positive vs Negative

5 > -3 5 is greater than -3
-8 < 2 -8 is less than 2

🏆 Competition

In a game, your score is +150 points and your friend's score is -50 points. Since +150 > -50, you are winning!
